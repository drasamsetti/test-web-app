Creating a theme


The users with coordinator role can create themes and has an option to restrict the theme visibility to his account.




Selecting Reviewers


Theme co-coordinator selects the reviewers for his theme. The coordinator can add/remove reviewers at any point of time, also while the ideas are being posted. Multiple reviewers can be chosen for a theme.



Other users can start posting ideas (with optional attachments) once the theme gets created. The screen below shows a theme just created with no ideas yet posted.
