Roles

Roles:


The Idea Generation Tool has role based access to all themes and ideas. The tool has 4 roles:


  1. User: This is the most basic role. It has the following rights:


    • View all themes and ideas on those themes which are public and related to his own account
    • Improve theme by posting ideas to the peer discussion
    • View ratings of the ideas visible to him
    • Put his own Innovations


  1. Coordinator: This role has all the rights of the User role plus the following rights:


  • Creating a theme
  • Select reviewers for his theme


  1. Reviewer: This role has all the rights of the User role plus the following rights:


    • Rate the themes allotted to him and give his comments to the theme coordinator


  1. Administrator: This role has all the rights of the Coordinator role plus the following rights:


    • Edit/Add/Delete Accounts
    • Edit/Add/Delete Projects
    • Edit/Add/Delete Categories
    • Manage User rights


  1. Account Administrator: Users with this role have the right to accept or reject new user registrations for those belonging to his account. There will be an account administrator for every account defined in the system. He can only approve a user's registration request. The rights of newly registered management will be managed by the usual tool administrator only.
